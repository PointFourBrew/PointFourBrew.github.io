Eyeball

Saison

Brewed 25th July 2020
  • Saison
  • 7.4% ABV
  • 31 IBU
  • Target OG: 1.068
  • Target FG: 1.012

Eyeball is our first attempt at a Saison. Primarily using pilsner malt, with munich malt, wheat, and oats as adjuncts. Hopped with Willamette for bittering, and Tettnanger and Saaz for aroma. We also added small quantities of cracked coriander seed and grated ginger near the end of the boil.

Using WLP566 - a highly attenuating saison yeast - it was fermanted at a temperature of 24Â°C for three weeks - we needed the fridge after that for our next beer so we've taken a risk and left the fermenter in ambient conditions for a bit longer as there was still yeast in suspension and we hadn't hit final gravity yet.

The plan is to secondary in a carboy for up to a month and then bottle into champagne bottles to allow for lots of carbonation - currently thinking for 3.5 volumes using sugar and US-05.

We're being very fast and loose with temperature control. Anecdotal evidence suggests that this is probably fine for the style, but having more temperature controlled space would be very helpful for consistent results.
